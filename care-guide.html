Chanderi Silk

Premium pieces

Washing

Dry clean recommended for best results. If hand washing, use very cold water with a silk-specific detergent. Wash quickly and gently ‚Äî do not soak.

Drying

Roll in a clean towel to absorb excess water. Lay flat to dry away from heat and direct light. Never wring.

Ironing

Low heat only, on the reverse side. Place a pressing cloth between the iron and fabric. Or use a steamer held at a distance.

Storage

Wrap in muslin or acid-free tissue paper. Store flat. Keep away from direct light to preserve the shimmer and colour.

Do

  • Handle with clean, dry hands
  • Air out after wearing
  • Treat stains immediately with cold water

Don't

  • Spray perfume directly on the fabric
  • Use hot water ‚Äî ever
  • Hang on wire hangers (causes stretching)
